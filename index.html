About Jay You

Jay You

Genre : Trap/Hip Hop

Kudakwashe William Rukuni (born October 4, 1994), who performs under the name Jay You, is a Zimbabwean trap / hip-hop artist / musician and lead member of the band South Samora. He is popularly referred to by fans and media as "Judge", the English word for "Mutongi Gava", a name he earned mostly because of his signature ittelectual capabilities in solving disputes. The name "Jay You" comes from his actions, "Mutongi", which means "Judge".
